Google Publish or Perish (https://web.archive.org/web/20120611102605/http://docs.google.com/?) is a new science writing tool that facilitates paper submissions. The tool was field-tested at the NIH and should be particularly valuable for open access and public access journals. Accepted papers are automatically added to your Researcher ID (https://web.archive.org/web/20120611102605/http://labs.researcherid.com/mashlets/rid/mashletsServer.jsp?rid=A-7225-2008&mid=CollaborationNetwork&cat=Map?) account.

For more information, read this (https://web.archive.org/web/20120611102605/http://network.nature.com/forums/nnbloggername/1523?) forum post by Matt Brown.
