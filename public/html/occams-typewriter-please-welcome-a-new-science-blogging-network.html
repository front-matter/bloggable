Last Friday the latest science blogging network officially launched: Occam’s Typewriter. The independent blogging network started out with eight bloggers and one guest blog, all of them well characterized by Bob O’Hara. Most of the bloggers have moved their blog from Nature Network, where I wrote next to them from 2007 until September this year.

Richard, Jenny, Stephen, Austin, Frank, Erika, Cath and Henry, I wish you all good luck with the new blogging network.
