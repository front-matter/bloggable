Cellular Therapy and Transplantation today published my paper Reference Management meets Web 2.0 (https://web.archive.org/web/20101016090740/http://dx.doi.org/10.3205/ctt-2010-en-000087.01?). Regular readers of this blog probably know about most of the things I talk about in the paper, but I hope it is a good introduction for those new to reference management.

The paper wouldn’t be possible without FriendFeed, because Claudia Koltzenburg – the Managing Editor of the journal and regular contributor to FriendFeed – contacted me after one of our discussions there.
